ﾟ ILEXI APPAREL ﾟ


ILEXI is a new-age apparel brand dedicated to comfort, freedom of expression and sustainability.




Born in Los Angeles, ILEXI captures the casual eccentricity of the city and its diverse mix of residents. They provide clothing items made from the finest fair-trade fabrics, ranging from jeans, sweatpants, skirts, sweatshirts, tops, and much more. Each of our genderless, basic pieces come in over 15 colors, hand-dyed and sewn by workers earning fair wages right in LA.
